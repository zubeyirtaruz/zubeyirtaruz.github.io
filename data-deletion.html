Footify – Data Deletion Instructions

At Footify, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to: taruztech@gmail.com with the subject line: “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. In the body of the email, please include:
    • Your full name
    • The email address associated with your Footify account
    • A clear request that you wish your data to be deleted

Processing Time

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion of your data within 30 days. After deletion, you will no longer have access to any data or content associated with your account.

Important Note

Certain information may need to be retained for legal, security, or regulatory purposes, but will no longer be used for any other reason.

If you have any further questions, please contact us at: taruztech@gmail.com
